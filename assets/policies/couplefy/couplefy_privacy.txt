COUPLEFY PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2025

IndieTap Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Couplefy mobile application (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information
- Relationship milestone dates and anniversaries
- Custom messages and quotes you enter
- Profile information (names, photos if provided)
- App usage preferences and settings

Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage analytics
- Crash reports and performance data

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the Couplefy service
- Send you relationship milestone notifications
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Provide customer support

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

- All personal data is stored locally on your device
- We do not upload your personal relationship data to external servers
- Anonymous usage analytics may be collected to improve the app
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Couplefy may integrate with:
- Apple App Store / Google Play Store (for app distribution)
- Analytics services (anonymous data only)
- Push notification services

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Couplefy is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
- Contact us about privacy concerns

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app.
